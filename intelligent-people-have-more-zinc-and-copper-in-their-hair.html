4.  A brass alloy contains 70 g copper and 30 g zinc. Find the % by mass of zinc.​


% by mass of Zinc : 30%

Further explanation  

The concentration of a substance can be expressed in several quantities such as moles, percent (%) weight/volume,), molarity, molality, parts per million (ppm), or mole fraction.  

Percent mass of the component shows the total mass of the component in the overall mass of a compound/mixture  

[tex]\tt \%mass~substance=\dfrac{mass~substance}{mass~mixture}\times 100\%[/tex]

Given

70 g Copper and 30 g Zinc

Required

% by mass of Zinc

Solution

mass of mixture :

= mass of Copper + mass of Zinc

= 70 g + 30 g

= 100 g

% mass Zinc :

= mass of Zinc : mass of mixture/alloy

= 30 g : 100 g

= 30%
