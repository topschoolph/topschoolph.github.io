14. what is the meaning of ppm​


Answer:

parts per million

This is an abbreviation for "parts per million" and it also can be expressed as milligrams per liter (mg/L). This measurement is the mass of a chemical or contaminate per unit volume of water. Seeing ppm or mg/L on a lab report means the same thing.

15. What is the % by mass and ppm of 45.0 g of HCL in 200.0 g of solution


Problem:

What is the % by mass and ppm of 45.0 g of HCL in 200.0 g of solution?

Given:

[tex]\text{mass of solute = 45.0 g}[/tex]

[tex]\text{mass of solution = 200.0 g}[/tex]

[tex]\text{solute = HCl}[/tex]

Required:

[tex]\text{percent by mass}[/tex]

[tex]\text{parts per million by mass}[/tex]

Solution:

For percent by mass

[tex]\text{percent by mass} = \frac{\text{mass of solute}}{\text{mass of solution}} × 100[/tex]

[tex]\text{percent by mass} = \frac{\text{45.0 g}}{\text{200.0 g}} × 100[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{percent by mass} = 22.5\%}[/tex]

For parts per million by mass

[tex]\text{parts per million by mass} = \frac{\text{mass of solute}}{\text{mass of solution}} × 10^{6}[/tex]

[tex]\text{parts per million by mass} = \frac{\text{45.0 g}}{\text{200.0 g}} × 10^{6}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{parts per million by mass = 225000 ppm}}[/tex]
