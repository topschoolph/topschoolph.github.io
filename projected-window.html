Question:

#1 What is the shortcut key in creating in a bin in the project panel?

#2 What is the shortcut key in maximizing or minimizing the project panel window?

Answer:

#1 To open a bin in place, Ctrl-double-click (Windows), or Command-double-click (Mac OS) the bin. To open a bin in its own floating panel, Alt-double-click (Windows), or Option-double-click (Mac OS) the bin

#2 To maximize a window, grab the title bar and drag it to the top of the screen, or just double-click the title bar. To maximize a window using the keyboard, hold down the Super key and press ↑ , or press Alt + F10 .
