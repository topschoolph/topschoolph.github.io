Answer:

91 162.97 (rounded off)

Step-by-step explanation:

Since this is 150 000 for both semesters then we should divide it:

150 000/2 = 75 000

First Year: 75 000

Second Year: 75 000 + (75 000 x 0.05) = 78 750

Third Year: 78 750 + (78 750 x 0.05) = 82 687.5

Fourth Year: 82 687.5 + (82 687.5 x 0.05) = 86 821.875
