9. 1. Ethyl alcohol has a chemical formula C2H60. What is the mass of 1.87 molethyl alcohol?​


PROBLEM SOLVING

Ethyl alcohol has a chemical formula C₂H₆O. What is the mass of 1.87 mol of ethyl alcohol?

Calculate the molar mass of ethyl alcohol.

Chemical Formula is C₂H₆O

Molar Mass of Carbon = 12.01 g/mol

2 • 12.01 = 24.02 g

Molar Mass of Hydrogen = 1 g/mol

1 • 6 = 6 g

Molar Mass of Oxygen = 16 g/mol

1 • 16 = 16 g

Molar Mass of ethyl alcohol = molar mass of C₂ + molar mass of H₆ + molar mass of O

Molar Mass of ethyl alcohol = 24.02 + 6 + 16

Molar Mass of Ethyl alcohol = 46.02 g/mol

Convert the number of moles to mass.

1 mol C₂H₆O = 46.02 g/mol

(1.87 g C₂H₆O)(46.02 g C₂H₆O / 1 mol C₂H₆O)

= 86.06 g C₂H₆O
