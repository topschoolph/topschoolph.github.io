19. elements of the story The legend of barangay japitan​


Answer:

The Legend of Barangay Japitan is a story that is rich in cultural and historical elements. The story has several key elements that make it captivating and interesting to read.

1. Location Setting - The story is set in Barangay Japitan, a fictional place located on the slopes of Mount Apo. The location is significant as it plays an important role in the story. The area is described as lush and fertile, and it provides the resources that the villagers need to survive.

2. Characters - The story features several important characters, including Datu Japitan, Inday Japitan, and the Bagani. The main character is Datu Japitan, who is regarded as the founder of Barangay Japitan. He was described as a wise and noble leader who worked hard to serve and protect his people.

3. Themes - The story also explores several themes that are still relevant today. These themes include bravery, unity, loyalty, respect for elders, and love for one's community.

4. Historical Perspective - The legend of Barangay Japitan connects the present with the past, providing valuable insights into the history and culture of the Philippines. The story highlights the importance of remembering our history and preserving our cultural heritage.

5. Supernatural elements - The legend of Barangay Japitan also features supernatural elements. These include the Bagani or village protector and the description of Datu Japitan's death, which was said to have been caused by an enchanted talisman.

6. Moral Lesson – The story ends with a moral lesson that teaches us the importance of being grateful for the things we have and to be united to build a strong community.

Overall, the elements of the story The Legend of Barangay Japitan provide an interesting insight into the diverse culture and rich history of the Philippines.


20. Is the element that has 1 electron in the second shell a rare gas or a metal?​


The element with 1 electron in the second electronic shell (level) is a metal.

~ More precisely, an alkaline earth metal (from the 2nd group of S.P.). The valence level is level 2 (or L level).

Rare (inert) gases do not have freé electrons on the last layer, they have stable configurations (doublet for He, respectively octet for the others - Ne, Ar, Kr, Xe).
