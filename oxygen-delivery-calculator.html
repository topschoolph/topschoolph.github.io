11. The delivery of oxygen and nutrients to the tissues of the body is accomplished through the ________ circulation.


Answer:

The delivery of oxygen and nutrients to the tissues of the body is accomplished through the Systemic circulation.

Explanation:

The systemic circulation provides the functional blood supply to all body tissue. It carries oxygen and nutrients to the cells and picks up carbon dioxide and waste products. Systemic circulation carries oxygenated blood from the left ventricle, through the arteries, to the capillaries in the tissues of the body.

23. 2. How do the heart and lungs work together in the delivery of oxygen?​


Answer:

The right side of your heart receives oxygen-poor blood from your veins and pumps it to your lungs, where it picks up oxygen and gets rid of carbon dioxide. The left side of your heart receives oxygen-rich blood from your lungs and pumps it through your arteries to the rest of your body.
