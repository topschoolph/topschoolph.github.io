What do nurses do in times of emergency?​

ER nurses care for patients who have suffered trauma, damage, or serious medical issues and require immediate care. Because these nurses operate in crisis settings, they must be able to identify the best strategy to stabilize patients and limit pain as rapidly as possible.

Nurses also oversee and coordinate the patient's plan of care, as well as monitor interdisciplinary patient care activities, in order to ensure quality patient care. Assure that nursing care duties are effectively allocated based on the degree of staff knowledge, skills, and capacities in relation to the patient's demands and acuity.
