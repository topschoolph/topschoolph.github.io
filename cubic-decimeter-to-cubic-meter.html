Cubic meter is the appropriate unit of measure for the volume of a container van.

About meter, square meters and cubic meters Meters or running meters (m)

The running meter or commonly called the meter is used to express the length. So if you measure length on a highway, for example, the unit used is the running meter

The running meter is a unit of length that has a length of 1 meter.

Square meter (m²)

A square meter or commonly written M² is a unit of area expressed in a square shape with a length of 1 meter and a width of 1 meter or it can also be said to be a square that has sides of 1 m x 1 m

Still remember with a square read the meaning of a square and the square formula

The size of square meters or M² is generally used to express areas with small sizes such as the area of land or buildings. As for large sizes, it is usually stated in KM²

example: 1 m x 1 m = 1 m2 (one square meter)

The square meter represents the unit of area for a 2D shape

Cubic Meters (m³)

In addition to running meters and square meters, the term cubic meters is also known. The cubic meter is used to express a unit of volume. So cubic meters are definitely used in 3D shapes.

1 cubic meter is a cube 1 m long, 1 m wide and 1 m high.

Note:

meters or running meters (m) = a unit of length
