12. 16. A sprinkler on a golf course fairway sprays water over a distance of 50 feet and rotates through an angle of 90°. What is the area of the fairway watered by the sprinkler? a. 1963 sq. ft. b. 1936 sq. ft. c.1933 sq. ft. d.1639 sq. ft. explain how to get answer pls.


The area of the fairway watered by the sprinkler can be found by using the formula for the area of a sector of a circle: (angle/360) * π * r^2, where angle is measured in degrees, and r is the radius of the circle.

In this case, the radius of the circle is the distance that the sprinkler sprays water, which is 50 feet. The angle is 90°.

So, the formula to find the area of the fairway watered by the sprinkler is (90/360) * π * 50^2 = (1/4) * π * 2500 = 625π sq. ft.

625π is approximately 1963 sq.ft, hence the answer is A. 1963 sq. ft.

25. 13. Two diagonals divide a square carpet into 4 congruent triangles. The baseof each triangle is 5 feet and the height is 2.5 feet. What is the area of theentire carpet?A. 18 sq. ft.B. 20 sq. ft. C. 25 sq. ft. D. 27 sq. ft.​


Answer:

Without bothering about the 4 triangles, you can easily get total area as 25 sq. ft.

How?

The shape of the carpet is square, the side is 5ft, so the area is simply 5*5 = 25 sq. ft.

But if you didn't notice this, let's compute it using the triangles;

Area of each triangle = ½bh

where

b = 5 ft

h = 2.5 ft

So,

Area of each triangle = ½bh

Area of each triangle = ½(5)(2.5)

Area of each triangle = 6.25 sq. ft.

So total Area = Area of each triangle x 4

Total Area = Area of each triangle x 4

Total Area = 6.25 x 4

Total Area = 25 sq. ft.
