Answer:

there are eleven (11) yellow beads

Step-by-step explanation:

the total number of beads is 60. 40% of 60 is 24, and 5/12 of 60 is 25. you add the blue and red beads, then subtract to the total to find the amount of yellow beads.

the simplification this problem is 60 — (24—25)

Answer:

11 yellow beads

Step-by-step explanation:

Just add the number of blue and red beads then subtract it to the total number of beads to know how many yellow beads are there.

Getting the number of blue beads.

[tex]60 \times 40\% \\ = 60 \times 0.4 \\ = 24[/tex]

Getting the number of red beads.

[tex]60 \times \frac{5}{12} \\ = \frac{300}{12} \\ = 25[/tex]

Now, let's get the number of munchers.

[tex]60 - (24 + 25) \\ = 60-49 \\ = 11[/tex]

Therefore, there are 11 beads that are yellow.
