3. on a number line, point M is located at 2 while point T is located at 9.if point A is also on the line and is between point M and T,what are the possible location of A?​


Answer:

Since A is between M and T on the number line, its location should be greater than 2 and less than 9. Therefore, the possible locations of A are all the real numbers x that satisfy:

2 < x < 9

In interval notation, we can write this as:

A ∈ (2, 9)

So, any point on the number line between 2 and 9, excluding the endpoints 2 and 9, could be a possible location for A.
