If a car is traveling at a constant speed of 80 km/h and the distance it needs to travel is 76 km, it will take approximately 57.5 minutes to reach its destination. To find this, simply divide the distance by the speed: 76 km / 80 km/h = 0.95 h = 57.5 minutes. Keep in mind that this is just an approximate answer, and the actual time it takes to travel this distance may vary depending on factors such as traffic, road conditions, and the type of vehicle being used.
