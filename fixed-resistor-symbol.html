Advantages of resistors

Resistors are very small. Hence, it is very easy to carry them from one place to another place.

Resistors are very cheap. Hence, it is easy to replace them.

Resistors do not depend on the external source of voltage. Hence, external voltage or energy is not needed for operating the resistors.

Disadvantages of resistors

Resistors with high resistance will oppose large amount of electric current. Hence, large amount of energy is wasted in the form of heat.

19. A two identical 15 kQ resistors are connected in series with a fixed 34 volt power supply. Find the current being supplied in the circuit. Find also the voltage on each resistor.​


[tex] \Large{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

Based on the problem, we have two find the current and voltage. When calculating for current we must use the formula:

[tex] \boxed{\bold{I = \frac{V}{R}}} [/tex]

While calculating for voltage, use the formula:

[tex] \boxed{\bold{V = I \times R}}[/tex]

Find the current being supplied in the circuit.

[tex] I = \frac{V}{R} [/tex][tex] I = \frac{34}{15} [/tex][tex] I = 2.266 [/tex]

Answer: [tex] \boxed{2.266} [/tex]

Find also the voltage on each resistor.

[tex] V = I \times R [/tex][tex] V = 2.266 \times 15 [/tex][tex] V = 33.99 [/tex]

Answer: [tex] \boxed{33.99} [/tex]
