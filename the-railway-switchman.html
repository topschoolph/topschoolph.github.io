23. What is the plot of the story of "By the Railway Side"?


the PLOT is the sequence of events in the story. it includes the exposition, rising action,climax,falling action and denouement.
1.the exposition is where the characters are introduced
2.the rising action is the part where the story starts to get complicated or the problem arises
3. the climax is the part where the thrill is at its highest.
4.the falling action is the part that introduces the solution to the problem
5. denouement is the last part/resolution
