1. how do health policies affect and protect citizenry


Any public policy made by the government will affect its citizens.
Health policies, for example, will affect each citizen differently. Some policies may be more beneficial to a group of people than others, while some may be good for the whole population, or some may add more expense for others.
One health policy you would like to check out is the benefit, through Philhealth, which was made free for all senior citizens and indigenous citizens. While this does seem good on paper because senior citizens or indigenous citizens usually do not have work or have no source of income, some may disagree that it is nota good allocation of public funds. Some think that it only perpetuates the notion of not saving or investing money while young for their own retirement, which is very rampant in the Philippines. For indigenous people, the application is automatically approved as long as you are under the acceptable income bracket, with no regards to whether you are part of an indigenous group or not.
