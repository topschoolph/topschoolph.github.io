1. what is the difference between a tensiometer and a hydrometer​


Answer:

Hygrometers and hydrometers are both measuring devices. The main difference between hydrometer and hygrometer is that a hydrometer measures the specific gravity (and therefore, the density) of a liquid whereas a hygrometer measures the humidity in the atmosphere.

3. what is diffrent between a tensiometer and a hydro meter​


[Answer]✍(◔◡◔)

As nouns the difference between tensiometer and hydrometer is that tensiometer is an instrument used to measure the moisture content of soil while hydrometer is an instrument that floats in a liquid and measures its specific gravity on a scale.
