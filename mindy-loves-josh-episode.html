24. mark and mindy are together 84 years old. 3 times marks age equals 4 times mindys age. how old are they


Let x - age of Mindy
84 - x - age of Mark

3(84 - x) = 4x
252 - 3x = 4x
4x + 3x = 252
7x = 252
x = 36 ---- Mindy
84 - x = 48 ---- Mark

To check

3(48) = 4(36)
144 = 144

36 and 48 are the ages of Mindy and Mark, respectively.
