24. What instrument is commonly used for measuring the mass of fruits, vegetable and meat?​


Answer:

Scale or Balance.

Step-by-step explanation:

A scale or balance is a device to measure the weight or mass of a fruit, vegetable and meat. These are also known as mass scales, weight scales, mass balances, and weight balances. Balances were equal arm balances. Balances have features including mass unit conversion, counting, percentage, limit functions for checking weighing, or specific gravity weighing. However, the difference is that scale measures weight while a balance measures mass. There are types of balances and this are spring balances, top loader balance, analytical balance, equal arm balance and portable balance.
