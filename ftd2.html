To find the area of the backyard, we need to find the area of the rectangle and the area of the right triangle, and then add them together.

The area of the rectangle is given by:

Area of rectangle = length x width

Area of rectangle = a x b

Area of rectangle = 21 ft x 32 ft

Area of rectangle = 672 ft²

The area of the right triangle is given by:

Area of triangle = (base x height) / 2

Area of triangle = (c x (b-a)) / 2

Area of triangle = (36 ft x (32 ft - 21 ft)) / 2

Area of triangle = (36 ft x 11 ft) / 2

Area of triangle = 198 ft²

The total area of the backyard is the sum of the area of the rectangle and the area of the right triangle:

Total area = area of rectangle + area of triangle

Total area = 672 ft² + 198 ft²

Total area = 870 ft²

Therefore, the area of the backyard is 870 square feet, which is closest to option C, 912 ft².

27. 9. Amy needs to order a shade for triangular-shaped window that has a base of 6 feet anda height of 4 feet. What is the area of the shade?A. 15 ft2B. 22 ft2C. 24 ft2D. 12 ft2​


AREA OF TRIANGLES

The area of the triangular-shaped window is 12 ft².

Triangle Definition

A triangle is a closed figure that has three vertices, three sides, and three angles. It is one of the most fundamental geometric shapes, and it is represented by the symbol △. Triangles in mathematics can be categorized according to their sides and angles into many categories.

Area of a Triangle Formula

Several formulas can be used to determine a triangle's area. When we know the lengths of all three sides of a triangle, for instance, we can apply Heron's formula to get its area. When two sides and the angle created between them are known, trigonometric functions can also be employed to determine a triangle's area.

However, the fundamental formula for calculating a triangle's area is as follows:

Area of triangle = 1/2 × base × heightA = 1/2 × B × H

Area of Triangle Using Heron's Formula

Think of the triangle ABC, which has sides that are a, b, and c long. Using Heron's formula, we can get the triangle's area:

Area = √(s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c))

Keep in mind that the triangle's perimeter is (a + b + c). The semi-perimeter, or "s," is defined as (a + b + c)/2.

GIVEN QUESTION

Amy needs to order a shade for a triangular-shaped window that has a base of 6 feet and a height of 4 feet. What is the area of the shade?

Be Discovered

base = B = 6 feet = 6 ftheight = H = 4 feet = 4 ft

Question

Area = A = ...

Solution

A = 1/2 × B × HA = 1/2 × 6 ft × 4 ftA = 1/2 ×24 ft²A = 12 ft²

Therefore, the area of the triangular-shaped window is 12 ft².
