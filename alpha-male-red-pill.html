23. PLEASE HELP!!A pharmacy technician measures the mass of a pill. The mass of one pill is 183mg. What is the mass in kilograms of 100 pills? Write your answer as a decimal.


SOLUTION:

Note that 1 g is equal to 1000 mg and 1 kg is equal to 1000 g.

[tex]\begin{aligned} \text{mass in kilograms} & = 183 \: \frac{\text{mg}}{\text{pill}} \times \frac{\text{1 g}}{\text{1000 mg}} \times \frac{\text{1 kg}}{\text{1000 g}} \times \text{100 pills} \\ & = \boxed{\text{0.0183 kg}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the mass in kilograms of 100 pills is 0.0183 kg.
