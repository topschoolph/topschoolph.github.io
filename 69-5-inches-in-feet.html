4. The distribution of heights of adult american men is approximately normal with mean 69 inches and standard deviation 2.5 inches. between what heights do the middle 95% of men fall?


Standard Deviation

The standard deviation reveals how far data deviates from the mean. While a high standard deviation indicates that the data are more spread, a low standard deviation suggests that the data are clustered around the mean.

Problem:

American adult men's height distribution is roughly typical, with a mean of 69 inches and a standard variation of 2.5 inches. What range of heights does the middle 95% of males inhabit?

Solution:

Step 1: 95%/2= 47.5%.

Step 2: Track down the z score that equals 47.5%. That comes out to 1.96.

Step 3. Determine the 95% confidence interval using the formula: 69±2.5*1.96 = 69±4.9.

Step 4: 69+4.9=73.9. 69–4.9= 64.1.

Therefore, the majority of 95% of men fall within the range of 64.1 and 73.9 or in the range of 64 – 74 inches in height.

Additional information:

Contrary to distance and average true range, variance is a measure of distribution that takes into consideration the distribution of all the data points in a data collection. It is the dispersion index that is most usually used, along with the confidence interval, which is simply the measure of dispersion.

21. Suppose that the heights of male basketball players are normally distributed with an average of 70 inches and a variance of 4. What is the probability that a randomly selected male basketball player has a height that is in the range 69 inches to 72 inches?


PROBLEM:

Suppose that the heights of male basketball players are normally distributed with an average of 70 inches and a variance of 4. What is the probability that a randomly selected male basketball player has a height that is in the range 69 inches to 72 inches?

SOLUTION:

Step 1: Identify the parts of the word problem. The word problem will identify:

The mean (average or μ) — 70 inches

Standard deviation (σ) — 2

Standard deviation is the square root of variance. [tex]\rm \sqrt{4} = 2 [/tex]

X: the numbers associated with “between” — 68 inches and 73 inches

The height is in the range 69 inches to 72 inches thus they are between 68 and 73 inches.

Step 2: Figure out the z-scores and find the area in the z-table. Plug the X values into the z value formula and solve. The μ (the mean), is 70 inches.

[tex]\small\underline{\boxed{z=\frac{X-μ}{σ}}}[/tex]

[tex]z=\frac{68-70}{2}[/tex]

[tex]z=\frac{-2}{2}[/tex]

[tex]z=-1=0.34134[/tex]

[tex]\small\underline{\boxed{z=\frac{X-μ}{σ}}}[/tex]

[tex]z=\frac{73-70}{2}[/tex]

[tex]z=\frac{3}{2}[/tex]

[tex]z=1.5=0.43319[/tex]

See z-table and the graph (illustration) on the attached pictures.

Step 3: Add the computed z-scores to get the probability or area between -1 and 1.5. You can also convert them in percent.

[tex]\boxed{0.34134+0.43319=0.77453=77.45\%}[/tex]

ANSWER:The probability that a randomly selected male basketball player has a height that is in the range 69 inches to 72 inches is 0.77453.
