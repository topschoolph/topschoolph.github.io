12. . 5. The denominator of a fraction exceeds its numerator by 4. If 6 is added to the numerator and 2 is subtracted from the denominator, the resulting fraction equals to 5. Find the fraction.


Answer:

[tex]\frac{-1}{3}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x as n,

The denominator of a fraction exceeds its numerator by 4.

Equation 1: [tex]\frac{x}{x + 4}[/tex]

If 6 is added to the numerator and 2 is subtracted from the denominator, the resulting fraction equals to 5.

Equation 2: [tex]\frac{x + 6}{x + 4 - 2 } = 5[/tex]

 [tex]\frac{x + 6}{x + 2} = 5[/tex]

Multiply both sides with ( x + 2 )

( x + 6 ) = 5( x + 2 )

( x + 6 ) = 5x + 10

Transpose and combine like terms,

-5x + x + 6 - 10 = -4x - 4

-4x/-4 = 4/-4

x = -1

Substitute to the equation 1:

[tex]\frac{-1}{( -1) + 4} = \frac{-1}{3}[/tex]

To check:

[tex]\frac{(-1) + 6}{(3) - 2} = 5/1 = 5[/tex]

23. If the numerator of a certain fraction is increased by 6 and it's denominator is decreased by 5 the resulting fraction is equal to 3/4. If the reciprocal of the original fraction is decreased by 1 then the resulting fraction is 16/9. Find the original fraction.​


Step-by-step explanation:

Here's the fraction,

[tex] \frac{a}{b} [/tex]

The first statement is:

[tex] \frac{a + 6}{b - 5} = \frac{3}{4} [/tex]

1. Let's simplify this,

[tex] \frac{a + 6}{b - 5} = \frac{3}{4} \\ \\ 4(a + 6) = 3(b - 5) \\ \\ 4a + 24 = 3b - 15 \\ \\ 4a - 3b = - 39[/tex]

From the 2nd statement, the reciprocal of the original fraction is

[tex] \frac{b}{a} [/tex]

Then,

[tex] \frac{b}{a} - 1 = \frac{16}{9} [/tex]

2.

[tex] \frac{b}{a} = \frac{16}{9} + \frac{9}{9} \\ \\ \frac{b}{a} = \frac{25}{9} \\ \\ 9b = 25a[/tex]

You can substitute this value in to equation 1 and solve for a

[tex]4a - 3b = - 39 \\ \\ 3(4a - 3b) = 3( - 39) \\ \\ 12a - 9b = - 117[/tex]

Now substitute, 9b = 25a

[tex]12a - 9b = - 117 \\ \\ 12a - 25a = - 117 \\ \\ - 13a = - 117 \\ \\ a = 9[/tex]

You can the back substitute to find b

[tex]9b = 25a \\ \\ 9b = 25(9) \\ \\ 9b = 225 \\ \\ b = 25[/tex]

Hence the original fraction is

[tex] \frac{9}{25} [/tex]
