[tex]Volume=s^3[/tex]
where s is the side length

So:
[tex]Volume=4.3^3=79.507[/tex]

Therefore the volume is 79.507 dm^3The formula for the volume of a cube is:
V=s³

Therefore, substitute the value:
V=(4.3 dm)³
V=79.507 dm³
