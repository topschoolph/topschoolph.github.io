3. The total mass of Heibi, Seedee and Wanfu is 235 kg. Heibi's mass is 2 times that of Seedee's and Wanfu's mass is 15 kg more than that of Seedee. What is the mass of Wanfu?​


Answer: 70 kgs

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = Heibi's mass

Let y = Seedee's mass

Let z = Wanfu's mass

"The total mass of Heibi, Seedee and Wanfu is 235 kg"

x + y + z = 235

"Heibi's mass is 2 times that of Seedee's"

x = 2y

"Wanfu's mass is 15 kg more than that of Seedee"

z = y + 15

x + y + z = 235

2y + y + (y + 15) = 235 (substitute formulas)

4y + 15 = 235

4y = 220

y = 55 (Seedee's mass)

z = y + 15

z = 55 + 15

z = 70 kgs// Wanfu's mass

Step-by-step explanation:

This can be explain by ratio and proportion. The ratio of box A is 2:3. The ratio of box B is 4:n. In this case, we have to find what is n.

2:3 = 4:n

To find n, multiply the numbers inside, and the numbers outside.

3 × 4 = 12

2 × n = 2n

With this, we can make a new equation.

2n = 12

Then, we can solve for n:

[tex]2n = 12\\\frac{2n}{2} =\frac{12}{2} \\n=6[/tex]

Thus, the answer is 6.
