9. What is Chemistry in Science?


Chemistry is the study of science that deals with matter, its properties, how it changes and why the changes occur. It aims to explain everything around us in the smallest of detail: the chemical composition of objects.

It helps us explain how fire works, how our bodies work in terms of chemicals, how your food tastes better if you put sugar or flavors on it. It is broad and can interconnect with other branches of science like biology and physics.
