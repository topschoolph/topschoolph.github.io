9. 2) Raffy is 4 years older than Addam. Next year Raffy will be 2 times old as Addam. How old Is Raffy? ​


Answer:

Let's represent Raffy's current age as "R" and Addam's current age as "A".

From the problem, we know that Raffy is 4 years older than Addam, so we can write:

R = A + 4

We also know that next year, Raffy will be 2 times as old as Addam, which we can write as:

R + 1 = 2(A + 1)

Now we can substitute the first equation into the second equation to solve for R:

(A + 4) + 1 = 2(A + 1)

A + 5 = 2A + 2

A = 3

So Addam is currently 3 years old.

We can use the first equation to find Raffy's current age:

R = A + 4 = 3 + 4 = 7

Therefore, Raffy is currently 7 years old.
