2. what is the difference between contamination and cross contamination?


Answer:

Contamination is the presence of substances and conditions in food that can be harmful to humans.

Cross contamination is the transfer of biological, physical or chemical contaminants to food products from raw foods, food handlers, and food processing equipment.
