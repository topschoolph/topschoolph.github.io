Market design is a practical process for establishing markets for certain properties that are based in part on mechanism design. Prices can be manipulated to elicit desired outcomes in some markets. Auction theory is the study of these markets.

Example:

Email layout.Designing for Social Media.Designing advertisements.Designing a landing page.Designing a websiteDesign for print.market strategy

A marketing strategy is a company's overall plan for reaching out to potential customers and converting them into paying clients for their goods or services. The company's value proposition, core brand message, statistics on target customer demographics, and other high-level elements are all included in a marketing plan.

Example:

Spotify is a service that provides a different type of user experience.Retargeting campaigns at Nordstrom.User-generated content (UGC) on GoPro.Nike's slogan is "Just Do It" and it promotes principles.market research

Market research is the process of actively interviewing potential customers to determine the viability of a new service or product. Market research enables a business to identify its target market and obtain comments and other input from customers about their interest in a product or service.

Example:

Interviews. Focus groups.Questionnaires.Surveys. market size​

The total number of prospective consumers of a product or service inside a specific market, as well as the total money that these sales may generate, is referred to as "market size." For a variety of reasons, it's critical to calculate and comprehend market size. Market sizing allows entrepreneurs and organizations to estimate how much profit they could make from a new business, product, or service. This aids decision-makers in determining whether or not to invest in it.
