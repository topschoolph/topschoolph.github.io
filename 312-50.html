30. A shop purchases 125 identical stuffed animals at a cost 312 and 50 cents and sell some for $7 each .whatpercent markup


Answer:

180%

Step-by-step explanation:

Cost per piece = 312.50/125 = 2.50

Markup = 7.00-2.50 = 4.50

Markup % = 4.50/2.50 = 180%

Checking:

2.50 x 180% = 4 50

Note: I just assumed that the markup% asked is based on cost. If the markup% is based on sales, my computation would be different.
