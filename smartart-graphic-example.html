4. What SmartArt graphic type is used to present data that createsan organizational chart?a. Listb. Cyclec. Hierarchy​


Answer:

C) Hierarchy

Explanation:

Smart Art is a special feature in MS Word that allows us to make different diagrams to organize and visually represent information. There are a multitude of different types, but let us focus on the ones you've mentioned.

List

Without numbers, it shows information in no particular orderWith numbers, it can show a process or steps to completing something

Cycle

Shows a continuous process, one that repeats over and over

Hierarchy

Shows levels between information often for organizational chartsShows a decision tree to organize decisions to be taken

Therefore, the best type would be Hierarchy.

SmartArt graphics is a visual presentation of your information or ideas. SmartArt graphics includes but not limited to diagrams and charts. With SmartArt graphics one can choose and create a layout that fits his or her information and or ideas.

ADVANTAGES OF USING SMARTART GRAPHICS

SmartArt graphics helps you visually represent a variety of concepts and ideas that might not work too well with just text.  

You can change between SmartArt graphics to explore what template works best to you.

Answer:

You can add beauty to your Microsoft Word documents by inserting a variety of "graphics".

"Graphics" allows you to visually communicate information rather than simply using text.

Clicking the "Insert" tab enables you to add pictures.

Word has a variety of SmartArt graphics you can use to illustrate and "organize" many different types of ideas.

You can select a required picture using the Insert Picture "dialog" box.

To get the most out of SmartArt, you'll need to know how to insert a SmartArt graphic, modify the "color" and "effects", and change the "layout" of the graphic.

Clicking the "Format" tab enables you to choose from numerous options to edit the graphics you have just inserted.

SmartArt graphics range from graphical "lists" and "process" diagrams.

"WordArt" provides a way to add fancy words in your Word document.

In formatting picture, "crop" enables you to remove unwanted parts of the picture.

In formatting picture, "Wrap Text" enables you to change the way the text wraps around the object.

In formatting picture, "Size" and "Position" enables you to change the width and height of the picture.

SmartArts are classified according to "type".

"List", "Process", "Cycle", "Hierarchy", "Relationship", "Matrix", "Pyramid", and "Picture" are different categories of SmartArts.

You can add a variety of shapes to your document, including "lines", "rectangles", "arrows", "stars", and "flowchart" shapes.

Word's large shape "library" allows you to organize and design the image you want.
