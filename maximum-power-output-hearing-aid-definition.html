8. what is the power output of a hydroelectric turbine that has a gross head of 50 meter and a head loss of 5 meter. The water flow rate is 100 cubic.meter/second and a turbine efficiency of 0.90 percent​


Answer:

P = 39,730,500W or 39.7305MW

Explanation:

[tex]P_{o} = QpgHn_{t}[/tex]

where:

Po = Power Output

Q = flow rate

p = density (water)

g = gravity

H = net head

[tex]n_{t}[/tex] = turbine eff.

Solution:

Solve for net head:

[tex]H = h_{g} - h_{l}[/tex]

[tex]H = 50 - 5[/tex]

[tex]H = 45m[/tex]

Solve for power output:

[tex]P_{o} = (100)(1000)(9.81)(45)(0.9)[/tex]

[tex]P_{o} = 39,730,500W[/tex]

A hearing test provides an evaluation of the sensitivity of a person's sense of hearing and is most often performed by an audiologist using an audiometer. An audiometer is used to determine a person's hearing sensitivity at different frequencies. There are other hearing tests as well, (e.g. Weber test and Rinne test.)
