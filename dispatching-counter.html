24. Three bus companies, V, W, and X, have the same route. Company V dispatches a bus every 12 minutes, company W every 16 minutes, aqnd company X every 32 minutes. If this bus companies dispatch their first bus at 4:00 a.m., what is the earliest possible time will the companies dispatch a bus simultaneously?


1) Find the LCM of 12, 16, and  32.
    LCM = 384

2)  Divide 384 minutes by 60 minutes to get the hour: 
     384 ÷ 60 = 6.4  
     6.4 = 6 hours and 4/10(60 minutes)
            = 6 hours and 24 minutes  or 06:24

3)  Add to 04:00  (4 am)
     04:00 + 06:24  = 10:24 a.m.

If the the three bus companies first trips is at 4:00 am, the next earliest possible time they'll dispatch their buses simultaneously (or the same time() is at 10:24 a.m.

ANSWER:  10:24 a.m.

Simple math.

We  she has a total off 100 colored counters

55 are reds

the rest are green or black

green are 4x than black

we can write it as,

100 = 55 + 4x + x

x will represent black while 4x will be black times 4 (or simply green).

Now time to compute.

move 55 to the left and change sign.

100 - 55 = 4x + x

Now add 4x and x and compute 100 - 55

we have,

45 = 5x

divide both 45 and 5x to 5.

(45 = 5x)/5

we have,

9 = x

therefore Sarah has a total of 9 black counters.

But your question is how many green counters she has?

So we need to multiply black counters to 4 to get it.

so we have,

9 * 4 = 36

so Sarah has a total of 36 green counters.

To check

we need to add all red, green and black counters. It should total to 100 colored counters.

55 + 36 + 9 = 100

55 + 45 = 100

100 ✔ 100
