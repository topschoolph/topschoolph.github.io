1250 computer chips are expected to be defective

Step-by-step explanation:

640 computer chips ÷ 8 defective computer chips = 80% chance of being defective

means 1:80 are defective

100,000 computer chips ÷ 80% chance of being defective = 1250 expected computer chips to be defective
