13. LearningAnswer the questions below. Express all your answers in scientific notationA human heart beats an average of 80 beats per minute,a. How many heartbeats is this in an hour?b. How many heartbeats is this in a day?c. About how many heartbeats is this in a year?d. About how many heartbeats is this in 80 years?​


a) 4.8 × 10³b) 1.152 × 10⁵c) 4.2048 × 10⁷d) 3.36384 × 10⁹

Step-by-Step Explanation:

Scientific Notation

Scientific notation is the method of writing very big or very small numbers in a short way. It is written as the product of a number and a power of 10. It is composed of three parts: coefficient, base and exponent.

Coefficient - It is the digit which must be greater than 1 and less than 10.

Base - It Is always 10.

Exponent - The number of places the decimal was moved to obtain the coefficient.

Let us now find the answers to the given questions above. Remember that you have to express the answer in scientific notation.

a. How many heartbeats are this in an hour?

Since there are 60 minutes in an hour, you have to multiply the average beats per minute which is 80 by 60.

80 × 60 = 4,800

Convert it to scientific notation. Remember that the coefficient should be greater than 1 but less than 10, so move the decimal place to the left. The number of moves you make is the exponent.

4,800 = 4.8 × 10³

b. How many heartbeats are this in a day?

Multiply the number of minutes there are in a day by 80. Remember that there are 24 hours in a day.

24 × 60 = 1,440 minutes

1440 × 80 = 115,200

Convert.

115,200 = 1.152 × 10⁵

c. About how many heartbeats is this in a year?

You already know that there are 115,200 heartbeats in a day. To find the answer, multiply it by 365 since a year is about 365 days.

115,200 × 365 = 42,048,000

Convert:

42,048,000 = 4.2048 × 10⁷

d. About how many heartbeats is this in 80 years?

You know that there are about 42,048,000 heartbeats in a year. To find the answer, multiply it by 80.

42,048,000 × 80 = 3,363,840,000

Convert.

3,363,840,000 = 3.36384 × 10⁹
