21. if the sum of the measures of the interior angles of a convex polygon is 3600°, how many sides does the polygon have?​


SIDES OF A POLYGON

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Problem:} [/tex]

if the sum of the measures of the interior angles of a convex polygon is 3600°, how many sides does the polygon have?

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Answer:}[/tex]

[tex]\qquad \qquad \huge \rm n = 22 [/tex]

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Solution:}[/tex]

Let say that our sum of the interior is 3600° let n be the sides of your polygon.

[tex]\bold{Formula : } \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad\\ \boxed{\rm{ Sum = (n - 2) \cdot 180° }} [/tex]

Substitute in your sum of the interior, dividby 180°. We know that the exterior and interior of an angle add up to 180° So therefore your solution is:

[tex]\rm 3600° = (n - 2) \cdot 180°[/tex]

[tex]\rm \frac{3600° }{180°} = n - 2[/tex]

[tex]\rm 20 = n - 2[/tex]

Let n be the total number of sides

[tex]\rm n = 20 + 2 [/tex]

[tex]\rm n = 22[/tex]

Therefore, your polygon have 22 sides.

Checking:

180°( n - 2 ) = 3600°180°( 22 - 2 ) = 3600°180°(20) = 3600°3600° = 3600°

Hence, Proven.

==================================
