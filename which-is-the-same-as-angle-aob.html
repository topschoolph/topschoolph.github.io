28. AOB and DOC are right angles. Name of all pairs of complementary angles​


Answer:

AOB and DOC are right angles. Name of all pairs of complementary angles​

Step-by-step explanation:

Two angles whose sum is 90° (that is, one right angle) are called complementary angles and one is called the complement of the other. Here, ∠AOB and ∠BOC are called complementary angles. ∠AOB is a complement of ∠BOC and ∠BOC is a complement of ∠AOB.
