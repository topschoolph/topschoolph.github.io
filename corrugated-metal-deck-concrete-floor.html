21. The swimming pool shown below has a 6 foot wide concrete deck around itWhat is the area, In square feet, of the deck around the pool?


Area of the deck = (w + 2 x 6 ft) x (l + 2 x 6 ft)

= (w + 12 ft) x (l + 12 ft)

= w x l + 12w + 12l + 144 sq ft

Therefore, the area of the deck around the pool, in square feet, would be (w x l) + 12w + 12l + 144 sq ft.

29. Why does it hurt more when you fall on a concrete floor than on a wooden floor?

Why does it hurt more when you fall on a concrete floor than on a wooden floor? ... Both floors provide the same impulse – the amount of force needed to bring your body to zero. If contact time is less for a concrete floor, the amount of force exerted must be greater to give the same impulse.

In this case, we can use the formula: F=fN

Where:

f is the frictional coefficient

N is the normal pressure

F is the frictional force
