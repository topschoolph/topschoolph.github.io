4. find the sum of odd integers from 1 to 100

What is arithmetic progression?  

• it is number sequence in which a constant figure is added to or subtracted from each number to give the next .

N=((l –a)/ d)+1

Given :

a is first number = 1

22. Find the sum of odd integers from 1 to 100


Answer:

The sum of arithmetic sequence of odd integers from 1 - 100, Sn = 2,500

Step-by-step explanation:

Question:

Find the sum of odd integers from 1 to 100

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

 Sequence of odd integers 1 – 100.

 

Required:

 Find the Sum of odd integers from 1-100

Solution:

To find for the sum of arithmetic sequence of odd integers 1-100.

                Let,

 Sn – sum of the arithmetic sequence

               a_1 – first term of the arithmetic sequence

                n  – total number of arithmetic sequence

              a_n – last term of the arithmetic sequence

    Using the formula in solving sum of arithmetic sequence,  

  Sn = n/2 [a_1+a_n ]

 Given values,

  n  = 50 , number of odd integers from 1-100

     where n=100/2=50

  a_1 = 1 , the first term of the sequence

  a_n = 99  , since the last odd number from 1-100 is 99.  

     

Applying values given in the formula,

 Sn = 50/2 [1+99]

     = (25)(100)

     = 2,500 is the sum of odd integers from 1-100.

24. the sum of all the odd integers from 1 to 100


We can describe all the odd integers from 1 to 100 as an arithmetic sequence with 1 as the first term(a1) and 2 as the common difference(d).
n = 50 (there are 50 odd integers from 1 to 100)
a1 = 1
d = 2
.
Formula for Arithmetic Series (and also the solution and answer):
Sn = n/2 [2a1 + (n-1)d]
S50 = 50/2 [2(1) + (50-1)2]
S50 = 25 [2 + (49)2]
S50 = 25 (2 + 98)
S50 = 25 (100)
S50 = 2,500
.
Therefore, the sum of all the odd integers from 1 to 100 is 2,500.

[tex]3 - 1 = 2[/tex]

a1=1

d=2

[tex] \frac{99 - 1}{2} + 1 = 50[/tex]
