5. The pitcher is 3 ¼ times bigger than a cup. If the cup is 4 ½ Fl oz, about how many Fl oz is the volume of the pitcher? A. 146.25 Fl ozB. 1.4625 Fl ozC. 14.625 Fl ozD. 68 5 Fl oz​


Answer:

C. 14.625 Fl oz

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's assume x is the volume of the pitcher, and y is the volume of the cup.

If the volume of the pitcher is 3 1/4 of that of the cup then the volume of the pitcher can be expressed as

x = (3 1/4)(y)

But from the problem, we are also given the value of the cup's volume y= 4 1/2 Fl oz. Substituting the value of y in the equation

x = (3 1/4)*(4 1/2)

x = 14 5/8 Fl oz or 14.625 Fl oz

The volume of the pitcher is 14.625 Fl oz

10. 3. Convert 640 milliliters (ml) to fluid ounce (fl oz)show solution po​


Answer:

The answer to the question how many ounces is 640 ml depends on the volume (oz) unit under consideration:

29.5735295625 ml = 1 US customary fluid ounce

28.4130625 ml = 1 Imperial fluid ounce = 1 UK fluid ounce = 1 Canadian fluid ounce

30 ml = 1 US nutrition labelling fluid ounce

Thus, 640 ml to oz is:

21.64 oz for United States customary fluid ounces

22.52 oz for Imperial fluid ounces = Canadian liquid ounces = UK liquid oz

21.33 oz for United States nutrition fluid ounces

Except for the food labelling ounce in the US, which is defined as 30 ml, the volume unit fl oz is derived from the gallon, 1 ⁄ 160 imperial gallons in the United Kingdom, and 1 ⁄ 128 gallons in the United States.

Convert 640 ML to OZ

Now let’s look at how to convert 640 ml to oz. In the UK and CA, there is only one fluid ounce, so changing 640 ml to oz UK or 640 ml to fl oz CA is a no-brainer.

Simply divide 640 by 28.4130625 using the formula [oz] = 640 / 28.4130625.

For United States customary fl oz divide the amount of 640 milliliters by 29.5735295625 to obtain the equivalent in ounces applying the formula [oz] = 640 / 29.5735295625.

And in the case of US food ounces just divide the volume in ml by 30 to get the volume in fluid ounces: [oz] = 640 / 30.

If you don’t know which type of US ounces you have, then note that the difference between 640 ml US customary oz and US food oz is only about 4 %.
