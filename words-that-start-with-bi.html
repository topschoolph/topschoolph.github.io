10. How much must you invest today in order to withdraw p2000 every 3 months starting at the end of the first period for 10 years if the rate of interest is 9% compounded bi-monthly?


Answer:

821.4

Step-by-step explanation:

A=P(1+(r/n)^nt

Wherein:

P= principal amt

R= rate

N= no. of times the amount be compounded

T= time

2000= P (1+ 0.09/4)^4(10)

2000= P (1.023)^40

2000= P (2.44)

2000/2.44= P

821.4= P
