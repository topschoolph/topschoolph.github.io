5. A characteristics of matter that wen describe.What is it?


Answer:

The definition of chemistry—the study of the interactions of matter with other matter and with energy—uses some terms that should also be defined. We start the study of chemistry by defining basic terms.

Physical properties are characteristics that describe matter as it exists. Some physical characteristics of matter are shape, color, size, and temperature. An important physical property is the phase (or state) of matter. The three fundamental phases of matter are solid, liquid, and gas

Chemical properties are characteristics of matter that describe how matter changes form in the presence of other matter. Does a sample of matter burn? Burning is a chemical property. Does it behave violently when put in water? This reaction is a chemical property as well (Figure  1.2.2 ). In the following chapters, we will see how descriptions of physical and chemical properties are important aspects of chemistry

A physical change occurs when a sample of matter changes one or more of its physical properties. For example, a solid may melt (Figure  1.2.3 ), or alcohol in a thermometer may change volume as the temperature changes. A physical change does not affect the chemical composition of matter.

Chemical change is the process of demonstrating a chemical property, such as the burning match in Figure  1.2.2  "Chemical Properties". As the matter in the match burns, its chemical composition changes, and new forms of matter with new physical properties are created. Note that chemical changes are frequently accompanied by physical changes, as the new matter will likely have different physical properties from the original matter.

What Is Alcohol?

The main psychoactive ingredient in alcoholic beverages is ethanol.

Generally referred to as “alcohol,” ethanol is the substance that makes you drunk.

It’s produced by yeasts that digest sugar in certain carb-rich foods, such as grapes — used to make wine — or grains — used to make beer.

Alcohol is one of the most popular psychoactive substances in the world. It can have powerful effects on your mood and mental state.

15. What is the meaning of the poem, Dead water by Wen Yi Tuo?​


Answer:

The creator of this poem was Wen yi-tuo, he is a Chinese and he was born September 15, 1946. He studied literature at United States of America. The Dead Water was published 1925 when he traveled back to china and he took a University teaching.

The Dead Water is all about our environment.This poetry was influenced by western models.During 1925 he joined the Cresent moon society and wrote essays. It has a A-B-C-B rhyme structure which is difficult to translate into English. This poem is an example of yi-tuo early political learnings, interpreting it as a bitter satire on the state of the government. The Dead Water is the poem employs a complicated rhythm structure of varying syllable length units. This poem was a interesting to explore, it is the idea of environmentalism. The second stanza the speaker states that the ditch could be redeemed by emerald grass and peach blossoms, but left to its own devices it only becomes a feeding area for the “gnats”who could be those who wish to feel upon the earth. The dead water will croak its song of delight which could be the tendencies of human towards self-destruction.

In this poem yi-tuo seems to be suggesting that there might be some beauty in ugliness, and asking if all dead things are truly hopeless. Because of the speaker attention to the detail the poem provides a useful overview of the topic the dead water.

27. Anong ang mga nagawa ni Tsai Ing-wen?(tagalog po pasagot po sana ng maayos​.


Explanation:

Si Tsai Ing-wen ay isang politiko mula sa Taiwan na nagsilbing Pangulo ng Taiwan mula 2016. Sa panahon ng kanyang panunungkulan, nakatuon siya sa ilang mahahalagang isyu, kabilang ang:

Pagpapatibay ng ekonomiya ng Taiwan: Pinatupad ni Tsai ang ilang mga patakaran sa ekonomiya na may layunin na mapag-innovate, mapataas ang mga export, at maakit ang panlabas na pag-invest. Dinidiskubre rin niya ang diversification ng ekonomiya ng Taiwan at binabawasan ang dependensya nito sa isang industriya o merkado lamang.

Pagpapabuti ng relasyon sa ibang mga bansa: Sinikap ni Tsai na mapabuti ang relasyon ng Taiwan sa ibang mga bansa at mapalawak ang internasyonal na presensya nito. Dinukwang rin niya ang mga diplomatikong relasyon ng Taiwan sa mga bansa sa buong mundo, kabilang ang Estados Unidos, Hapon, at Unyong Europeo.

Pagsusulong ng katarungan sa panlipunan at kapantay-pantay: Nakatuon si Tsai sa mga isyu ng katarungan sa panlipunan at kapantay-pantay, kabilang ang kapantay-pantay sa pagitan ng lalaki at babae, mga karapatan ng LGBTQ, at mga karapatan ng mga indiginiho. Dinukwang din niya ang pagpapabuti ng access sa edukasyon at healthcare, at pagbabawas ng kahirapan at income inequality.

Pagtugon sa pandemya ng COVID-19: Sinilangan ni Tsai ang tugon ng Taiwan sa pandemya ng COVID-19, kabilang ang pagpapatupad ng mga hakbang upang makontrol ang pagkalat ng virus at magbigay ng suporta sa mga naapektuhan ng pandemya.

Sa pangkalahatan, sinusubaybayan ng panunungkulan ni Tsai ang pokus sa economic development, international relations, at social justice.


28. The sum of Wen and Rod's age is 48, Rod is twice Wen's age,What is Rod's Age?


Answer:

32 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Wen = x

Rod = 2x

So your equation is: x + 2x = 48

Solving for x:

x + 2x = 48

3x = 48

x = 48/3

x = 16

Rod's age is 2(16) = 32 years old.
