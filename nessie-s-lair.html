Subject-Verb Agreement  

If the subject in the sentence is singular, use a singular verb. Add -s or -es to the verb.

Examples:

The little boy plays hide and seek with his friends after school.

Toyota launches brand new cars every year.

My brother helps me with all my home works.

If the subject in the sentence is plural, use a plural verb. Use the base form of the verb or without -s or -es.

Examples:

The little boys and girls play hide and seek together after school.

My brother and sister help me with all my home works.
