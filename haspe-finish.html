Definition:

9. D. Umbrella Nail – or Roofing Nails, are used in roof installation to fasten shingles, to install roofing felt for waterproofing and to attach roof tiles and sheet metal.

10. D. Wood Screw – A pointed metal screw formed with a sharp thread of comparatively coarse pitch for insertion in wood.

11. A. Carriage Bolt – A form of bolt used to fasten metal to metal or, more commonly, wood to metal.

12. D. Hinge – A movable joint or mechanism on which a door, gate, or lid swings as it opens and closes or which connects linked objects.

13. C. Hasp – A slotted hinged metal plate that forms part of a fastening for a door or lid and is fitted over a metal loop and secured by a pin or padlock.

14. D. Plywood – a structural material consisting of sheets of wood glued or cemented together with the grains of adjacent layers arranged at right angles or at a wide angle.

15. Baseboard Molding – A piece of wood molding that runs along the bottom of a wall.
