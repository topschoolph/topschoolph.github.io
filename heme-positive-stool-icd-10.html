7. illustrate and describe the structure of heme group?​


A heme is made from 4 pyrroles, which are small pentagon-shaped molecules made from 4 carbons and 1 nitrogen. Four pyrroles together form a tetrapyrrole. If the tetrapyrrole has substitutions on the side chains which allow it to hold a metal ion, it is called a porphyrin. Thus, a heme is an iron-holding porphyrin.

11. The top of a rectangular stool has an area of 27 square centimeters and a perimeter of 24 centimeters. What are the dimensions of the top of the stool?


A rectangle has an area of l*w and a perimeter of 2l + 2w.

l*w = 27cm²

2l + 2w = 24cm

2l = 24 - 2w

l = 12 - w

Substitute l in l*w.

l*w = 27

(12 - w)(w) = 27

12w - w² = 27

0 = w² - 12w + 27

0 = (w - 9)(w - 3)

w = 9, 3

The dimensions are 9cm x 3cm.

18. it can be due to constipation or diarrhea hard dry stools on one day and loose watery stools on another


Answer:

Severe constipation can cause a blockage in your bowel. Because of this, the bowel begins to leak out watery stools around the blockage from higher up in the bowel. The leak from the bowel can look like diarrhoea. It's called 'overflow diarrhoea.

If you have diarrhea, you'll also have loose or watery stools. However, if you have loose stools from time to time, it doesn't mean you have diarrhea. In order for loose stools to be considered diarrhea, they have to occur repeatedly. If you have loose stools three or more times per day, then it's diarrhea.
