25. A condensed account; a summary; person or thing typical of something a. Modus Operandi b. Faux pas c. Epitome d. Rendezvous


Epitome

Answer:

A condensed account; a summary; a person or thing typical of something is called Epitome. Meaning, an example of a quality, from the Greek word “Epi” or in addition and “Temnein” or to cut. An example of this word in a sentence is: “Those clothes are the epitome of modern style in today’s fashion level”. “She is the epitome of the well-known family in the Northwest”. “To think the epitome of the great civilization were once near a cave mine”.
