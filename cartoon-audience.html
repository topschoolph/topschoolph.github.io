26. Differentiate editorial cartoons (caricature) vs cartoon


Answer:

Editorial Cartoon, also known as a political cartoon, is an illustration containing a commentary that usually relates to current events or personalities.

Caricature a likeness that captures a person’s physical traits and simultaneously exaggerates them for humorous effect.

Cartoon aisual art form consisting of hand or computer-drawn images, often (but not always) created for humorous effect.
