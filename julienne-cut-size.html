1. proper way of julienne cut​


Answer:

1. take the knife into your dominant hand and hold it comfortably, using your non-dominant hand as a guide to the knife

2. holding the vegetables with your fingertips, bend your fingers so that they are parallel to the knife (this also protects them)

3. move your hand the same distance between each cut.

4. start slowly and as you gain experience, you'll be able to cut in a smooth continuous motion.
