13. what is the past progressive and future progressive tense? how are this tenses used ?​


What is the future progressive tense?

Future progressive tense indicates an ongoing action that will take place in the future

Example:

We will be flying over New York at noon tomorrow.

What is the past progressive tense?Past progressive tense indicates an ongoing action in the place that occurred before other past actions

Example:

She had been for an hour.

Additional:

Future progressive tense includes "will be" before the present participle of the verbPast progressive tense includes the past tense of "had" and past participle of "to be" before the present participle of verb

EXPLANATION:PAST PROGRESSIVE TENSE-To construct a sentence using past progressive tense, use the formula  ( "was" or "were" + [present participle] )PRESENT PROGRESSIVE TENSE

-Use the formula ( "am," is," or "are" + [present participle] )

FUTURE PROGRESSIVE TENSE

-Use the formula ( "will be" + [present participle] )

WHAT IS PRESENT PARTICIPLE?-The suffix '-ing' is added to the basic form of the verb to form the Present Participle. We use the Present Participle along with a form of the verb 'to be' to create continuous (progressive) tenses.
