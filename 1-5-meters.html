3. find the avaragr length 1 1/2 meters 1 2/5 meters and 2 1/2 meters​


Answer:

To find the average of the lengths 1 1/2 meters, 1 2/5 meters, and 2 1/2 meters, you need to add up the lengths and divide by the number of lengths.

First, we'll convert the mixed numbers to fractions.

1 1/2 meters = 3/2 meters

1 2/5 meters = 7/5 meters

Now we can add the lengths:

3/2 + 7/5 + 5/2 = (35 + 72 + 52)/(25) = 38/10 = 19/5 meters

Now we can divide by the number of lengths to find the average:

19/5 meters / 3 = 19/15 meters

So the average length of 1 1/2 meters, 1 2/5 meters, and 2 1/2 meters is 19/15 meters.

It is also possible to convert the fraction back to mixed number 19/15 meters = 1 4/15 meters
