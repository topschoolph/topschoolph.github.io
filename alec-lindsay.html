Answer:

4.1 meters

Step-by-step explanation:

Pythagorean theorem, a^2 + b^2 = c^2, 8 meters is a or b (base) and 9 meters is the c (hypotenuse) c^2 - a^2 = b^2 = 9^2 - 8^2 = b^2 = 9 = 81 - 64 = 17
