4. What is the value of 0.04 in fraction A.4/10 B.4/100 C.04/100 D.


Answer:

b. 4/100

Step-by-step explanation:

0.04 as a fraction is 4/100 which can be reduced to 1/25.

B. 4/100
To convert a Decimal to a Fraction follow these steps:
Step 1: Write down the decimal divided by 1, like this: 0.04/1
Step 2: Multiply both top and bottom by 100. (because there are 2 digits after the decimal point so that is 10×10=100).
0.04x100 = 4
1x100 = 100
= 4/100
Step 3: Simplify (or reduce) the fraction.
4/4 = 1
4/100 = 25
