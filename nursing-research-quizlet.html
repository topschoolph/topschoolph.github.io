16. Crrate/Research for a product service that you as a future nurse would like to venture into.​


Home health care services are becoming increasingly popular in the Philippines as an alternative to hospital-based care, particularly for patients who require ongoing medical attention or assistance with activities of daily living. These services include skilled nursing care, wound care, physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy, among others.

A future nurse could start a home health care service business by offering these services to patients in the comfort of their own homes. This could include collaborating with physicians to develop individualized care plans for patients, hiring and training licensed nurses and therapists, and providing medical equipment and supplies.

By offering home health care services, a future nurse could help address the growing demand for accessible and affordable healthcare services in the Philippines, particularly in rural areas where access to medical facilities is limited. Additionally, it could provide a more personalized and patient-centered approach to care, improving patient outcomes and satisfaction.


17. Explain in 5-8 sentences the interrelatedness or interdependence of nursing theory, research & practice.


Answer:

Theories are a set of interrelated concepts that give a systematic view of a phenomenon (an observable fact or event) that is explanatory & predictive in nature.

Theories are composed of concepts, definitions, models, propositions & are based on assumptions.

Theory gives planners tools for moving beyond intuition to design and evaluate health behavior and health promotion interventions based on understanding of behavior.[Robert T. Croyle (2005)].

They are derived through two principal methods; deductive reasoning and inductive reasoning. Nursing theorists use both of these methods.

Theory is “a creative and rigorous structuring of ideas that projects a tentative, purposeful, and systematic view of phenomena”.

A theory makes it possible to “organize the relationship among the concepts to describe, explain, predict, and control practice”
