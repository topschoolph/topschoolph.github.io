English/Western calligraphy mostly required a flat-edged pen to achieve the result while the Chinese used a flexible hair brush. Because the writing utensil was different, the outcome varied also. For example, a stroke from a pen was lighter than a stroke from a brush dipped in ink.

12. how to play Chinese garter English please​


Chinese garter are done in a minimum of three people where a thin garter is used, held by two people the one is the one to jump over the garter. It is done by level, first, the height starts at your ankle and as you are able to successfully jump over it, it gets more difficult with the height of the garter held horizontally getting higher. If you fail to jump over the garter, the next player will then take his/her turn.
