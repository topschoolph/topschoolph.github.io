13. A gas mixture consists of 3 gases with the following pressures: 150kPa He, 370kPa 0, and545kPa Nz. Calculate the total pressure of the gas mixture.​


Calculating the total pressure inside the container.

Given

Pressure of Helium (P₁) = 150 kPaPressure of Oxygen (P₂) = 370 kPaPressure of Nitrogen (P₃) = 545 kPaTotal Pressure (Pₜ) = ?

According to the Dalton's Law of Partial Pressures, the total pressure is the sum of the partial pressures in the container.

Pₜ = P₁ + P₂ + P₃

Pₜ = 150 + 370 + 545

Pₜ = 1065 kPa

16. If 150.00 mL of Nz gas was collected at 760 torr, what is the new volume of thegas when the pressure is compressed to 740 tor at the same temperature?NEED HELP​


Answer

The new volume is 1.23 mL

Explanation:

Given:

P1 = 760 torr

P2 = 740 torr

V1 = 150 mL

Required: V2

Solution:

P1V1 = P2V2

V2 = P1V1 ÷ P2

V2 = (760 torr) (150 mL) ÷ (740 torr)

V2 = 1.23 mL
