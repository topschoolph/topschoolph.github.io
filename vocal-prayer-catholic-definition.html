23. how is Christian morality related to our Catholic prayer and worship​


Answer:

Christian morality helps us discover how we should live our lives as a result of our faith in God's word which ... THE FIRST COMMANDMENT.

Answer:

The basis of all Catholic Christian morality is our belief in the God who created all things on thisworld. We believe we are created in God’s image and that we, and all creation, are basically good. Yet we recognize our own tendencies toward evil, especially in an excess of our desires. The Ten Commandments are part of the code known to the early Israelites that helped them to live better lives in relationship with God. We believe in the same values, with certain changes because of our knowledge of Jesus Christ.
