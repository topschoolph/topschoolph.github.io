7. english riddles with answers


ENGLISH RIDDLES WITH ANSWERS

Riddles are usually difficult, misleading, or puzzling questions that are constructed as a tricky problem to be solved or guessed.

1.) Question: If ten birds are sitting in a tree and a hunter shoots one, how birds many are left in the tree?

Answer: None, because after one bird got shot, the rest flew away.

2.) Question: What did the quilt say to the bed?

Answer: I’ve got you covered.

3.) Question: Which room has no walls?

Answer: A mushroom

4.) Question: The thing that is sticky and brown?

Answer: A stick

5.) Question: What has a head, a tail, but does not have a body?

Answer: A coin
