2. What is the largest perfect cube factor of 54


Cube Root of 54

The value of the cube root of 54 rounded to 7 decimal places is 3.7797631. It is the real solution of the equation x3 = 54. The cube root of 54 is expressed as ∛54 or 3 ∛2 in the radical form and as (54)⅓ or (54)0.33 in the exponent form. The prime factorization of 54 is 2 × 3 × 3 × 3, hence, the cube root of 54 in its lowest radical form is expressed as 3 ∛2.

What is the Cube Root of 54?

The cube root of 54 is the number which when multiplied by itself three times gives the product as 54. Since 54 can be expressed as 2 × 3 × 3 × 3. Therefore, the cube root of 54 = ∛(2 × 3 × 3 × 3) = 3.7798.

How to Calculate the Value of the Cube Root of 54?

Cube Root of 54 by Halley's Method

Its formula is ∛a ≈ x ((x3 + 2a)/(2x3 + a))

where,

a = number whose cube root is being calculated

x = integer guess of its cube root.

Here a = 54

Let us assume x as 3

[∵ 33 = 27 and 27 is the nearest perfect cube that is less than 54]

⇒ x = 3

Therefore,

∛54 = 3 (33 + 2 × 54)/(2 × 33 + 54)) = 3.75

⇒ ∛54 ≈ 3.75

Therefore, the cube root of 54 is 3.75 approximately.

Is the Cube Root of 54 Irrational?

Yes, because ∛54 = ∛(2 × 3 × 3 × 3) = 3 ∛2 and it cannot be expressed in the form of p/q where q ≠ 0. Therefore, the value of the cube root of 54 is an irrational number.

12. The total surface area of a cube is 54 square centimeters. How long is its edge?


Answer:

a = 3 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the surface area of a cube comprises six identical squares, the S.A. of a cube with edge length a is:

SA = 6[tex]a^{2}[/tex]

We know the SA is 54 [tex]cm^{2}[/tex] and we want to find a, so we plug in what we know and solve for what we need:

54 = 6[tex]a^{2}[/tex] ⟹ [tex]\frac{54}{6}[/tex] = [tex]a^{2}[/tex] ⟹ 9 = [tex]a^{2}[/tex]

⟹ ±3 = a

However, the length of the edge of a cube can’t be negative, so a = 3 cm.
