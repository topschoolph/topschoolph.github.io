In a powerpoint presentation, the slides can be viewed as thumbnails either in the Normal View, which is the default view.

In the normal view, the thumbnails of slides are visible on the left-hand side pane.

Another view that shows thumbnails, is the slide-sorter view, that shows the thumbnails in the main view pane.

Both the views can be opened using buttons on the bottom right-hand side of the powerpoint window.

Thumbnails in either views help us to overview our presentation along with other controls like adding,deleting, and changing order of slides.
