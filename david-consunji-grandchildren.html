17. Grandma Howard evenly split $256 among her 8 grandchildren. Five of the grandchildren want to combine their money to get something. How much do the 5 grandchildren have together?


Answer:

the 5 grandchildren have $160 together.

Step-by-step explanation:

Grandma Howard split $256 evenly among her 8 grandchildren, which means each grandchild received:

256 / 8 = 32

So each grandchild received $32.

If five of the grandchildren want to combine their money, they would have a total of:

5 x 32 = 160

Therefore, the 5 grandchildren have $160 together.

[tex]324 \div 9 = 36[/tex]
[tex]36 \times 3 = 108[/tex]
Answer:

D. $108

1 grandchild = 324 ÷ 9 = $36


1 grandchild = $36

3 grandchildren = 36 x 3 = $108
