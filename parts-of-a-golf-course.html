25. A sprinkler on a golf course fairway is set to spray water over a distance of 70 feet and rotates through an angle of 120°. Find the area of the fairway watered by the sprinkler. A. 5131ft2 B. 4200ft2 C. 73.304ft2 D. 294000ft2


Answer:

A. 5131ft^2

Step-by-step explanation:

Area of a sector = 1/2(r^2)θ

Given: r= 70ft, θ=120°

Required: A=?

Equation: A= 1/2(r^2)θ

Solution:

*θ must be in radians

120 ° *( π/180 °) =  2 π/3

A= 1/2(70ft^2)(2π/3)

A= 4900 π/3 ≈ 5131ft2

30. When a golf ball is hit, it travels at 41 meters per second the mass of a golf is 0. 045 kg what is the kinetic energy of the golf ball?.


Explanation:

KE=½mv²

KE = ½(0.45kg)(41m/s)²

KE = ½(0.45kg)(1681m²/s²)

KE = ½(756.45m²/s²•kg)

KE = 378.225m²/s²•kg
