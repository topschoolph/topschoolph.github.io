18. A certain laser pointer gives light with a wavelength of 632 nm. What is the color of this light? What is its energy?


Answer:

a. The color of the light is red

b. The energy of the wavelength is 3.15 x 10⁻¹⁴ J

Explanation:

To know the color of the given wavelength, we have here a color spectrum chart with its corresponding wavelength.

Color                Wavelength range (nanometer, nm)

Red                             625 - 740

Orange                       590 - 625

Yellow                        565 - 590

Green                         520 -565

Blue                            500 - 520

Indigo                          435 - 500

Violet                          380 - 435

Therefore, 632 nm wavelength is within the range of 625 - 740 nm the color is red.

Now, let us solve the energy of this wavelength using the formula

E = hc / λ

where

E     is the energy, the unit is in Joules (J)

h     is Planck's constant, 6.626 x 10⁻³⁴ J/s

c     is the speed of light, 3 x 10⁸ m/s

λ     is the wavelength, the unit is in meter, m

Solving the problem

Let us use the formula and substitute the given data

E = hc / λ

E = (6.626 x 10⁻³⁴ J/s)(3 x 10⁸ m/s) / 632 x 10⁻⁹ m

E = 3.15 x 10⁻¹⁴ J

Therefore, the energy of 632nm wavelength is 3.15 x 10⁻¹⁴ J
