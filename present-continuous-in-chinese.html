5. Pursue your analysis on the presence of Chinese warships in the West Philippine Sea, create a tabular presentationof your internal and external criticism on the matter​


Answer:

One of the greatest challenges facing the country today is how to defend and preserve our sovereign rights in our Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) in the West Philippine Sea (WPS). What is at stake is a maritime area larger than the total land area of the Philippines, and this huge maritime area is rich in fish, oil, gas, and other natural resources.

I remember in early 1995 when China seized from the Philippines Mischief Reef, a submerged atoll within the EEZ of the Philippines in the WPS. I was then the Chief Presidential Legal Counsel of President Fidel Ramos. The Philippines was totally defenseless. We did not have the military capability to take back Mischief Reef. We could not invoke the PH-US Mutual Defense Treaty because Mischief Reef is outside the territory of the Philippines. We could not go to a tribunal under the United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea or UNCLOS because China had not yet ratified UNCLOS at that time. This incident left a deep scar in my mind – that a powerful state could just grab what legally belonged to a weaker state, and there was no power on earth that could right such a wrong.

Answer:

Ang mga Chinese mestizo ay may malaking kontribusyon sa kasalukuyang ekonomiya ng Pilipinas. Mula pa noong panahon ng Kastila, ang mga Tsino ay naging mahalagang bahagi ng ekonomiya ng bansa. Ang kanilang pagdating ay nagdulot ng pagbabago sa ekonomiya at kultura ng Pilipinas.

Sa kasalukuyan, ang mga Chinese mestizo ay may malaking papel sa pagpapalago ng ekonomiya ng bansa. Marami sa kanila ang nagtatayo ng mga negosyo at kumikita ng malaki. Sila ay nagbibigay ng trabaho sa maraming Pilipino, kabilang na ang mga manggagawa at propesyonal.

Ang mga Chinese mestizo ay kilala sa kanilang sipag at tiyaga sa pagtatayo ng negosyo. Sila ay may mataas na antas ng kasanayan sa pagpapatakbo ng mga negosyo at hindi takot sa pagkuha ng mga risgo. Sila ay may kakayahang mag-adapt sa iba’t ibang sitwasyon at maghanap ng mga oportunidad upang mapalago ang kanilang mga negosyo.

Bukod sa pagkain at kalakalan, ang mga Chinese mestizo ay may malaking kontribusyon din sa sektor ng real estate. Marami sa kanila ang may-ari ng mga lupa at gusali sa mga kanayunan at lungsod. Sila ay nagtatayo ng mga condominiums, mga shopping mall, at mga office building.

Sa kabuuan, ang mga Chinese mestizo ay may malaking kontribusyon sa ekonomiya ng Pilipinas sa kasalukuyang panahon. Sila ay nagdulot ng pagbabago sa ekonomiya at kultura ng bansa. Ang kanilang sipag at tiyaga, kasanayan sa pagpapatakbo ng mga negosyo, at kakayahang mag-adapt sa mga sitwasyon ay nagdulot ng mga oportunidad para sa mga mamamayan ng Pilipinas. 28. What product did britain continue to export despite objections by the chinese government? silk fabric opium coal art?


Despite objections by the Chinese government, the British government continued to import opium, a highly addictive substance to China throughout most of the 18th and 19th centuries.

The Opium Trade was deemed a necessity by Britain and other European countries as a way to mitigate the trade imbalance between them and China. Europe and the British empire had a massive demand for most Chinese luxury products such as silk, porcelain, and tea but China had no such demand for European merchandise.

The trade was so robust that opium addiction soon became a pervading problem in China. The Chinese government tried to enforce steps to limit the trade but it merely resulted in the Opium Wars, two armed altercations fought between China  and Britain.

The first Opium War of 1839 -1842 made the Chinese government concede and cease efforts to stop the opium trade. The second Opium War 1856-1860 fought between the Chinese and a British-French coalition was a major defeat to the Chinese as it forced them to legalize the opium trade.
