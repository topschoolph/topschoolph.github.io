19. A boy lifts a 30N dragon 2 meters above the ground. How much work did the boy do on the dragon?


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The work done by an object exerting a force F at a distance d is given by

[tex]\boxed{W = Fd}[/tex]

where:

W = work

F = force

d = distance

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for W

W = Fd

W = (30 N)(2 m)

[tex]\boxed{W = \text{60 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the work done is 60 J.
