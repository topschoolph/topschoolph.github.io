29. on your PE notebook Anwer the following questions1. What is festival dances?2. What are the 2 types of festival Dance?3. Give 3 examples of each type (festival dance)4. Why do we have featival dance?5. Give the meaning of each word.a. festivalb. percussionc. bountifuld. honore. patron​


1. Festival dances are traditional dances that are performed during festive events or celebrations.

2. The two types of festival dance are ceremonial and social.

3. Examples of ceremonial festival dances are:

- Haka (New Zealand)

- Samba (Brazil)

- Bhangra (India)

Examples of social festival dances are:

- Salsa (Latin America)

- Polka (Europe)

- Line dancing (Western countries)

4. Festival dances are performed to celebrate and honor cultural traditions, to express joy and happiness, and to bring people together in a spirit of community and cooperation.

5.

a. Festival - a celebration or occasion of cultural or religious significance

b. Percussion - musical instruments that are played by striking, shaking, or scraping

c. Bountiful - abundant, plentiful, or generous

d. Honor - respect, admiration, or recognition for someone's achievements or qualities

e. Patron - a person who supports, sponsors, or funds an organization or activity.
