Answer:

There are a couple of ways to interpret this statement, but one possible meaning is:

1 yard = 3 feet

To convert yards to feet, we can multiply by 3:

1 yard = 3 feet

1 × 3 = 3 feet

So 1 yard is equal to 3 feet.

If we want to know how many feet are in 240 yards, we can use multiplication:

1 yard = 3 feet

240 yards = 240 × 3 feet

240 yards = 720 feet

Therefore, there are 720 feet in 240 yards.

28. A person is planting one garden bed that is 15 feet in length and another that is 9 feet in length. If there are 3 feet in 1 yard, what is the total length, in yards, of the two garden beds combined?


Answer:

The total length of the two garden beds combined is:

(15 feet + 9 feet) ÷ 3 feet/yard = 8 yards

Answer:

The total length of the two garden beds in feet is 15 + 9 = 24 feet.

To convert feet to yards, we need to divide the total length by the conversion factor of 3 feet per yard:

24 feet ÷ 3 feet/yard = 8 yards

Therefore, the total length of the two garden beds combined in yards is 8 yards.
